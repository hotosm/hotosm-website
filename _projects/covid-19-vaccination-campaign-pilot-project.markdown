Americas: Haiti
West Africa: CAR, Cameroon, Chad, Niger, Burkina Faso
East & S. Africa: Somalia, Ethiopia, South Sudan, Sudan, DRC
Asia-Pacific: Afghanistan, Bangladesh, Yemen
Other: Syria, Iraq
